About Us

A.S. Accessories Company started to be in the apparel accessories field since the year 2009. We have earned a reputation of being a reliable supplier of the highest quality of apparel accessories joining hands with our valuable manufacturers within a very short period of time. We supply most of the regular items such as zippers, buttons (in Metal and polyester), threads, elastics, webbings, draw-codes, stoppers, buckles, eyelets, fashion belts etc.



Our Vision

The vision of our company is, to develop it to the maximum level where we can build up a group of companies in other countries too, and produce our accessories in our own manufacturing plants



Our Strengths

As suppliers of an extensive range of products we serve our customer requirements with quality products, fast and efficient service at cost effective prices. We are well supported by a closely knit network of reliable manufacturers with whom we have collaborations in that we super head the promotion and the marketing of their product.

Our office serves our customers with the best service and reasonable prices.





Quality of materials

All our partner companies produce high quality goods that are above the required quality standards. The raw materials used by our manufacturers, are environment friendly and non hazardous to the end user. High tech and Advanced state of the art machinery being used which are upgraded from time to time, enables us to undertake and execute large orders within a short period of time. We also enjoy the benefit of special price the advantage of which we willingly pass on to you.






Our Customers

We have an extensive range of customer base in Sri Lanka. We have developed and built a strong lasting business relationship with all our customers through our dedicated, reliable and personalized service rendered throughout the passed year. Some prestigious names our customers’ process orders for include Gap, Tesco, Marks and Spencer’s, May Department, Columbia, Target Stores and so on.
